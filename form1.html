Prospective international students are expected to have a suitable tuition budget (average of $15,000 - 25,000) to commence their study application processes.

There are no application fees charged by schools in the UK and Australia. To ensure commitment to your study goals. A REFUNDABLE deposit fee maybe be applicable. Terms and conditions apply.

Application fees ranging from $50 - 300 are applicable to schools in Canada and some schools in the USA. These application fees are charged by schools on applications and remitted to them once paid.
